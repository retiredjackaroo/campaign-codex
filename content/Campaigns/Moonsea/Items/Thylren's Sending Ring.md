# Thylren's Sending Ring

A one-way sending ring carried by [[Thylren Saj]]: the [[Zhentarim]] can receive but not reply. [[Welsea Parenthe]] wants it retrieved if he is compromised.

**First appears:** [[Chapter 11 - Episode 1 - Session 1 The Silver Pyramid]]
