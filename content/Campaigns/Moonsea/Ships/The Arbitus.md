# The Arbitus

A long, shallow-drafted cargo keelboat captained by [[James Cel]]. The party's transport upriver, now the site of the boarding fight with [[Vurthak]]. Recorded in Roll20 as "The Arbutus"; also referred to in session as "The Arbitus" -- both retained as aliases.
