# Cassra Brandywine

A cheerful, ink-stained halfling and [[Pug D'Astibar]]'s former supervisor at [[Mantor's Library]], recorded in Roll20 as Scribe Master Cassra Brandywine. Also referred to at the table as Kastra Brandywine; both names are retained as aliases. Survived the fall of the city and delivered [[The Lord Sage]]'s summons to the war council.

**Status:** Alive
**First appears:** [[Chapter 11 - Episode 1 - Session 1 The Silver Pyramid]]
