# Yarish

A transmutation archmage of [[Phlan]]'s founding era. His reckless experiments on [[Sorcerer's Isle]] corrupted its waters and wildlife; he was slain by [[Wren O'The Blade]]. His pyramid still stands.

**Status:** Deceased\
