# Fenrik

![[med-6.png]]
Freed from imprisonment at [[The Lyceum]]. Stayed in [[Phlan]] alongside [[Levith]] to organise resistance.

- Speaks politely, softly, often apologetically

- Treats violence as _an unfortunate business necessity_

- Refers to murders as “clean-ups” or “rearrangements”

- Genuinely enjoys good tea and gossip

**Status:** In Phlan\
**First appears:** [[Chapter 11 - Episode 1 - Session 1 The Silver Pyramid]]
