# Church of Shar

The faith of Shar, goddess of darkness, loss, secrets and the night. Shar is the twin and eternal nemesis of [[Church of Selune|Selune]], and her church tends toward secrecy and nihilism, drawing in the grieving and the embittered.

[[Iltiza Ward|Tiz]], a devoted Selunite, is an enemy of the church, which holds a standing order to neutralise her.
