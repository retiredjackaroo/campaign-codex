# Church of Selune

The faith of Selune, goddess of the moon, of navigation and wanderers, and of hope and guidance through darkness. Selune is the eternal foe of her twin [[Church of Shar|Shar]], and her church stands for compassion and light against loss and despair.

[[Iltiza Ward|Tiz]] is a devoted follower, and serves the church as a High Priest (rank to confirm); she works in a temple of Selune during downtime.
