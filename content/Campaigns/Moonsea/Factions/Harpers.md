# Harpers

A scattered, secretive network of spies, scouts and idealists who work from the shadows to oppose tyranny and the abuse of power, and to keep a balance between civilisation and the wilds. They prize individual freedom and the quiet trade of information over open force, operating in small cells rather than as an army.
