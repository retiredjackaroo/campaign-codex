# Emerald Enclave

A widespread order devoted to the balance of the natural world and to helping people survive in the wild places. In the Moonsea region they are represented by [[Seranolla the Whisperer]], the shaman of the grove at the edge of [[The Quivering Wood]], who serves as [[Petra Hillockburrow|Petra]]'s contact.
