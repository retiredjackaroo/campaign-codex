# Yarish's Pyramid

The pyramid built by [[Yarish]] on [[Sorcerer's Isle]]. Its interior is a maze that reconfigures as you move through it; no party has returned from it since [[Wren O'The Blade]]'s time.

**First appears:** [[Chapter 11 - Episode 1 - Session 1 The Silver Pyramid]]
