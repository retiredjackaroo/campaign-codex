# Melvaunt

A rigid, guild-run [[Moon Sea]] city. Welcomes refugees who forsake [[Phlan]] and become citizens, then charges a fee to leave: hostage-taking by another name.

**First appears:** [[Chapter 11 - Episode 1 - Session 1 The Silver Pyramid]]
