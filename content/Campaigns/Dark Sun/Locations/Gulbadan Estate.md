# Gulbadan Estate

![[Pasted image 20260627220322.png]]
The estate and seat of House Gulbadan, the faction that ruled [[Raam]] under [[The Padshah]], with [[The Gulbadan Maulani]] as Grand Vizier. [[Tchk'Tchk Gulbadani]] took the Gulbadani name as his own when the party entered the house's service, tying his chosen identity to this lineage. Since the [[Raam|Inundation of Raam]] killed [[The Padshah]], the house is headless and its standing, and the name Tchk'Tchk carries, is in jeopardy.

**Status:** Headless since the Inundation of Raam; future uncertain
