# The Plaza

![[Pasted image 20260628013808.png]]
A plaza within [[Raam]], currently serving as a field camp for a coalition of [[The Templars]], [[Noble House Soldiers]], and [[Dregoth Cultists]]. The camp consists of tents, bonfires, and trash fires, active with patrols moving in and out as well as guards stationed throughout.

Beneath the plaza lies the secret entrance to [[The Cisterns]], the underground network used by [[The Alliance]].

**Status:** Occupied by enemy field camp
**City:** [[Raam]]
**First appears:** <!-- link to Episode 11 once built -->

## Strategic notes

Three routes were available to the party during Episode 11 for moving [[Leviath]] and fifteen peasants through or past the plaza:

- Through the plaza under fog or obscurement, risking enemies lifting the fog
- The close cistern entrance, accessed via the plaza itself
- The further cistern entrance, accessed through [[The Slums]]
