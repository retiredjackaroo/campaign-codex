# Raam

![[Pasted image 20260627220114.png]]

The city-state in which the campaign has been based throughout. Raam is one of the city-states of Athas, ruled until recently by [[The Padshah]] as head of the Gulbadan faction, with [[The Gulbadan Maulani]] serving as Grand Vizier. The city suffered a catastrophic event known as the Inundation of Raam, during which the Padshah died.

The party has operated across multiple districts and locations within Raam, navigating a complex web of factions including [[The Templars]], [[Noble House Soldiers]], [[Dregoth Cultists]], [[The Wardens]], [[The Alliance]], and the remnants of Gulbadan power.

**Status:** Damaged (Inundation of Raam)
**Former ruler:** [[The Padshah]] (deceased)
**First appears:** Episode 1

## Factions operating in Raam

- [[The Gulbadan Maulani]] - former ruling faction, now headless
- [[The Alliance]] - hidden resistance below the city
- [[The Templars]] - sorcerer-king enforcers
- [[Noble House Soldiers]] - noble house military presence
- [[Dregoth Cultists]] - followers of the undead sorcerer-king
- [[The Wardens]] - martial faction
- [[The Venerants of Dust]] - religious faction at [[The Furnace]]

## Key events
