# The Skull

![[Pasted image 20260628014120.png]]
A skull recovered from the remains of the sand elemental defeated in [[The Cisterns]] during Episode 11. The skull was originally thrown into the aqueduct by [[Dregoth Cultists]] after they detected the party descending through the outer well. It animated into a huge sand elemental, pulling stone and sand into itself over time — the longer the party had taken to descend, the larger it would have grown.

On the elemental's defeat the skull fell free from the collapsing mass. [[Her]] moved in and took it from the wreckage.

**Status:** In party possession\
**Origin:** Dregoth Cultists\
**First appears:** [[Episode 11 (Overview)]]

## Notes

Significance unknown. The fact that the Dregoth Cultists used it as a weapon suggests it may be a reusable or repurposable tool of elemental summoning. Worth investigating.
