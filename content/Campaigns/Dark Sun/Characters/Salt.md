# Salt

![[Pasted image 20260627141001.png]]

A Psion who presents himself to others as human. Salt claims to be human and most of the party have no reason to doubt this. His true nature as a Water Genasi is not common knowledge within the group. On Athas, where water is the most precious resource on a dying world, a being of elemental water would be remarkable — and potentially dangerous to reveal.
