# The Templars

Agents of the sorcerer-kings of Athas, the Templars serve as the administrative and enforcement arm of the city-state's ruler. On Athas, Templars draw their magic directly from their sorcerer-king, making them both bureaucrats and dangerous spellcasters. They are a constant threat to anyone operating outside the law.

During Episode 11 a contingent of Templars formed part of the field camp occupying the plaza above [[The Alliance]]'s hidden network, with patrols moving in and out of the camp. Their discovering the cistern entrance would be catastrophic for the resistance below.
