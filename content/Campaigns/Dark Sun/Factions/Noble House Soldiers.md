# Noble House Soldiers

Soldiers serving one or more of the noble merchant houses of the city-state. On Athas the noble houses wield enormous economic and political power, and their private armies are a significant force. Their presence alongside [[The Templars]] and [[Dregoth Cultists]] in the plaza field camp suggests a convergence of interests, or at minimum an uneasy alliance of convenience, against a common target.
