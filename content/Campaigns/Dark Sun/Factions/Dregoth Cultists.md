# Dregoth Cultists

Followers of Dregoth, the undead sorcerer-king of Giustenal, a city-state destroyed long ago and now a ruin beneath the earth. Dregoth himself is a dracolich of immense power who survived his assassination and now plots his return and revenge against the remaining sorcerer-kings. His cultists pursue his agenda on the surface world.

Their presence in the plaza field camp alongside [[The Templars]] and [[Noble House Soldiers]] makes them one of three hostile factions the party must navigate in Episode 11.
