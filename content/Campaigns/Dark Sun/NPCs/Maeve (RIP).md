# Maeve

![[Pasted image 20260627123818.png]]
[[Derfel]]'s second Primal Companion: a dog, and the second of three. Maeve died in Episode 10, killed by two ranged attackers who targeted her while the party was rescuing children. She had already bitten the primary target before they brought her down. She went out fighting.

**Status:** Deceased (killed in action, Episode 10)\
**Preceded by:** [[Dave (RIP)]]\
**Succeeded by:** [[Brave]]

## Death

Maeve was focused by two ranged attackers during a combat in Episode 10. She fell in two rounds. [[Derfel]] and [[Her]] took their revenge. The children were rescued.

She had bitten the big bastard first.

## Relationships

- [[Derfel]]: devoted companion until the end
- [[Her]]: Her grief at Maeve's death was wordless, as all her grief is, and all the heavier for it
