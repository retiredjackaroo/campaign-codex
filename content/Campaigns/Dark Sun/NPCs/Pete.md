# Pete

The Dungeon Master of Born Under a Dark Sun. Architect of Athas as the party experiences it. Responsible for every patrol, every ambush, every ranged attacker who has ever looked at a dog and seen an opportunity.

Goes by Ereni Lokestar at the table. Known to his victims as Pete.

**Status:** Omnipresent and dangerous\
**Affiliation:** Ostensibly neutral. Evidence suggests otherwise.

## Character notes

Pete maintains a professional pretence of impartiality while demonstrating a consistent and targeted interest in the welfare of [[Brave]], [[Maeve]], and [[Dave]]. The pattern of three coordinated ranged attackers focusing [[Maeve]] in Episode 10 is either a remarkable coincidence or a declaration of intent. It is not a coincidence.

Despite this, the cruelty is purposeful rather than random. Pete uses dogs as a narrative lever with precision: the tension generated serves the story, the revenge arc serves the characters, and the table is better for it. This does not make him innocent. It makes him effective.

## Known methods

- Coordinated ranged attacks on companion animals
- Morally complex scenarios designed to stress-test party cohesion
- Detailed tactical briefings that are definitely not dissuading anyone from anything
- Rescuing children at the cost of dogs

## Verdict

An excellent DM and a menace to dogs everywhere.

**First appears:** Episode 1, presumably.
